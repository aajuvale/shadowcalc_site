Privacy Policy

ShadowCalc

Last updated: July 7, 2026

ShadowCalc is a calculator app. It does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All data in Phantom Countdown is stored locally on your device. None of your data is shared with the app developer or any oter entities; furthermore, it is managed entirely by the end user.

Data Collection

ShadowCalc does not collect any personal information, usage data, or analytics. The app does not require an account and does not connect to any server.

Data Storage

Your settings, such as your selected theme and haptic feedback preference, are stored locally on your device and are never shared with anyone, including the developer.

Purchases

ShadowCalc offers an optional tip jar to support development. Purchases are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store, and no payment information is collected or seen by ShadowCalc. All features of the app remain free regardless of purchase.

Third Parties

ShadowCalc does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Contact
